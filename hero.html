Be a Hero: Comics for Social Change

Kapow!

Comics in schools can help make illiteracy a thing of the past. We donate 2% of every comic book sale to programs such as those in our inner-city public schools through age-appropriate comic book donations to libraries. We believe every kid deserves to be a hero!

Bam!

Take that, linguistic confusion! Cosmic Comics also donates to language prorams throughout the country. comics provide ESL students the unique juxtaposition of visual elements and text to help learning a language and a culture become quick, lasting, and even fun!

Zap!

No more must learning disabilities get in the way of a kid's ability to imagine herself as a hero. Our comic book donation program also targets programs for dyslexia, visual processing disorder, and similar reading challenges.
